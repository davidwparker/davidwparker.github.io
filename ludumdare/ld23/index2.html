Tiny God - A Ludum Dare 23 Tiny World Game

Backstory

You are a tiny God of Tiny Worlds.

You want to be a big God.

All of the Tiny Worlds are misbehaving. It's time to destroy them all.

Rules

Tiny God is a turn-based game.

Click on Tiny Worlds so that you can cause miracles and expand your total belief.

As your belief expands, you can continue on to bigger worlds. Over time, belief in your miracles subsides, so you must go back to previous worlds where you performed miracles.

Press h anytime for help.
